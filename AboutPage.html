ABOUT PETER'S

Our Location:

Peter’s Pizzeria is located on 40 Ossipee Road, Somerville, where our chef and all of our staff live in house.

Our Mission

Peters Pizzeria is a local homemade nonprofit pizzeria dedicated to uniting peers, friends, and the Tufts and Summerville communities with excellent homemade Italian pizza. All money made from serving pizza gets put back into quality ingredients, back into the pizza, back into the community that we love.


Our Chef

PETER MORGANELLI has studied the art of pizza since before he could walk. He has dedicated ours and made sacrifices for the love of pizza that could make any grown man cry. His warm pies also could make any grown man cry.


Our Staff

The staff members are all local Tufts students. The kitchen is staffed by Jonah, Gus, Ezra, Harrison, and Hayes.
